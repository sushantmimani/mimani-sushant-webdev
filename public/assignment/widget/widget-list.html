For us, the espadrille (pronounced “ess-puh-drill”) is the embodiment of old-school cool — especially those bearing the name Rivieras. Inspired by the ones worn along the Spanish and Italian Rivieras in the 1950's, the brand’s Classic Slip-On ($60) is built for summer.

Their low-profile silhouette is crafted in Spain from durable canvas, lined with soft terrycloth, and given a natural gum rubber outsole. All this makes an exceptionally durable and versatile casual shoe — one that compliments a pair of chinos just as well as your favorite boardshorts.
